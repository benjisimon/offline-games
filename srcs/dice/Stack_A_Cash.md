# Stack A Cash

## Setup

Create a 3x3 board like tic-tac-toe

Gather or make 6 'coins': 2 large, 2 medium and 2 small.

Each player gets 1 of each coin type.

One player plays heads, one player plays tails.

All six coins are common to both players.

## Game Play

On your turn you can:

* Place a coin with your side up
* Move and/or flip a coin

Coins can only be stacked big to small.

You cannot move or flip a coin that was just moved, flipped or placed.

## Winning

The game ends as soon as someone makes three in a row of the same side (heads or tais) on the topmost layer of coins.
