# Bottle Pool

## Goal

Be the first player to reach exactly 0 points.

## Setup

* Each player starts with 15 points
* Rack all 15 balls
* Decide who breaks

## Game Play

* Take turns shooting
* Sink any ball to subtract its value from your score
* Keep shooting until you miss
* If you scratch, add 2 points to your score

## Scoring

* Ball sunk = subtract that ball's number from your score
  * 1-ball = -1 point, 7-ball = -7 points, etc.
* Scratch = +2 points
* Going below 0: you "bust" — add back the value of the last ball you sunk and end your turn

## Conclusion

First player to reach exactly 0 wins. You must hit exactly 0 — busting keeps you in the game.

## Tips

* Play conservatively near 0 — only shoot balls whose value matches what you need
* Use scratches to your advantage early when the points don't matter as much
