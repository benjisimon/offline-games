# Straight Pool

## Goal

Be the first player to sink 5 balls.

## Setup

* Rack all 15 balls in the triangle
* Decide who breaks (youngest goes first, or flip a coin)

## Game Play

* Break the rack
* Take turns shooting
* On your turn, sink any ball — stripes and solids don't matter
* Each ball sunk = 1 point; keep shooting until you miss
* If you scratch (cue ball falls in a pocket), your turn ends and you lose 1 point

## Scoring

* 1 ball sunk = 1 point
* Scratch = -1 point
* First to 5 points wins

## Conclusion

Fast games, easy to chain rounds. Play best of 3 or first to win 3 rounds.
