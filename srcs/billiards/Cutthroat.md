# Cutthroat

## Goal

Be the last player with at least one ball still on the table.

## Setup

* 3 or more players
* Rack all 15 balls
* Assign ball groups after the break:
  * 3 players: 1–5, 6–10, 11–15
  * 4 players: 1–4, 5–8, 9–12, 13–15 (last group has 3)
  * 5 players: 1–3, 4–6, 7–9, 10–12, 13–15
* Groups are assigned in order of who shoots first — you don't get to pick

## Game Play

* Break the rack; the breaker chooses their group after the break
* Take turns shooting at any ball on the table
* You want to sink your opponents' balls, not your own
* Each player's turn continues as long as they keep sinking balls
* Miss: turn passes to the next player

## Scratching

* If you scratch (cue ball in a pocket), each opponent may return one of their sunk balls to the table

## Elimination

* A player is eliminated when all of their balls are sunk
* An eliminated player can return if an opponent scratches — their ball comes back

## Conclusion

Last player with at least one ball on the table wins.
