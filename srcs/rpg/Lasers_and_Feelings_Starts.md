# Lasers & Feelings — Opening Scenarios

Roll or pick. Each is a one-sentence situation to hand the crew at the start of play.

Roll 2d6 to pick: first die = section (1–5, reroll 6), second die = scenario within section (1–5, reroll 6).

---

## Section 1: Rescue & Recovery

1. A distress beacon leads to a derelict Consortium cruiser. Everyone aboard is
   in stasis — but something ate through the hull from the inside.

2. A famous explorer's escape pod is found drifting. She's alive, but claims
   she's only been gone three days. Records show she's been missing forty years.

3. A distress call comes from a planet under quarantine. The caller is a
   Consortium officer. The quarantine is classified.

4. A small moon is on a collision course with a populated planet. The crew has
   six hours.

5. A space station needs evacuation. It has room for 200. There are 400 people.

---

## Section 2: Alien Encounters

1. Alien ships have surrounded the Raptor. They're not attacking. They seem to
   be waiting.

2. An alien delegation boards the ship wanting to negotiate — but they'll only
   speak to Captain Darcy, who is in a coma.

3. An alien offers to show the crew "the most important place in the galaxy" —
   but the ship has to go alone.

4. An alien child latches onto the ship. Its parents are coming. They are very
   large.

5. The Raptor drops out of warp in the middle of a battle between two fleets,
   neither of which recognizes the ship.

---

## Section 3: Ship Problems

1. Something has been eating the ship's energy crystals from the inside. The
   fuel gauge is dropping fast.

2. The ship's AI declares an emergency and locks everyone out of the bridge.
   It will only talk to one crew member.

3. An alien artifact in cargo activates at midnight and won't stop
   transmitting.

4. Something is following the Raptor through warp space. It's faster than
   anything known.

5. The ship receives a message from Captain Darcy — time-stamped six months
   in the future.

---

## Section 4: Mysteries

1. A neutral station claims the Raptor fired on them. You didn't. The evidence
   says otherwise.

2. A crew member wakes up speaking a language no one recognizes — including
   themselves.

3. The Raptor's sensors detect a planet that isn't on any chart — and it's
   broadcasting on a Consortium frequency.

4. A pirate captain hails the ship. She looks exactly like one of the crew
   members.

5. The Raptor encounters a ghost ship crewed entirely by androids who believe
   the war ended 200 years ago.

---

## Section 5: Pressure & Choices

1. A Consortium general orders the crew to destroy an unarmed civilian
   freighter. No explanation given.

2. A trade convoy asks for escort — then turns out to be smuggling something
   the Consortium desperately wants destroyed.

3. The crew discovers Captain Darcy's personal log contains entries made after
   he went into the medical pod.

4. The crew is hired to escort a scientist to a restricted sector. She won't
   say what's there.

5. A child stowaway is found in cargo. She says she's from a colony that was
   destroyed three years ago — but she looks exactly the same age as the
   survivor photos.
