# Honey Heist

## Premise

It's HoneyCon 2017. You are going to undertake the greatest heist the world
has ever seen.

One: You have a complex plan that requires precise timing.
Two: You are a GODDAMN BEAR.

## Character Creation

Roll 3D6 — one for each column:

**Descriptor**
1. Fearless / 2. Crafty / 3. Lucky / 4. Bold / 5. Quick / 6. Legendary

**Role**
1. Muscle / 2. Brains / 3. Driver / 4. Hacker / 5. Thief / 6. Face

**Bear Type / Special Skill**
1. Grizzly (Terrify) / 2. Polar (Swim) / 3. Panda (Eat anything that looks like bamboo)
4. Black (Climb) / 5. Sun (Sense Honey) / 6. Honey Badger (Carnage)

Name your bear if you want. You're not a talking bear, per se, but you can
sort of mangle human speech through your bear mouth, maybe.

## Disguise

Pick a Lego minifigure. That's your bear in disguise. Name them if you want.

## Sidekick

Pick a creature from the build pile. Describe one thing it's good at — that's
its power. Keep it in front of you next to your minifig.

Examples: *fast enough to outrun anyone*, *small enough to fit through vents*,
*scary enough to clear a room*.

## Stats

Each player has **two dice** in front of them — no math needed.

* **BEAR die** — starts at 3. Use for: maul stuff, run & climb, shrug off
  damage, scare people, bear stuff.
* **CRIMINAL die** — starts at 3. Use for: anything not directly related to
  being a bear.

When a stat changes, just flip the die to the new number.

## Rolling

**Step 1 — Pick your stat:** Is this a BEAR thing or a CRIMINAL thing?
Use the matching die to find your target number.

**Step 2 — How many dice?** Start with 1D6. Roll 2D6 and pick the lowest if:

* You're using your **bear special-skill**, OR
* You're doing something related to your **role**, OR
* Your **sidekick's power** applies

**Step 3 — Roll:** Equal to or under your stat = **success**. Over = **fail**.

## Changing Stats

The GM can shift any player's stats at any time — one person or everyone at
once. Two reasons:

**Frustration** — the plan is falling apart and the bears are losing it:
move one point from CRIMINAL into BEAR (flip both dice).

**Greed** — a phase of the heist went smoothly and the bears are feeling
clever: move one point from BEAR into CRIMINAL (flip both dice).

Players can also shift their own stats voluntarily:

* **BEAR → CRIMINAL**: do a flashback scene planning the heist over coffee
  and cigarettes in the back room of a seedy bar.
* **CRIMINAL → BEAR**: eat a load of honey.

## The End

* If CRIMINAL reaches 6: you are lured into a life of crime and betray the party.
* If BEAR reaches 6: you flip out bear-style and lose it. Presumably picked
  up by animal control in half an hour or so.

## GM Setup — Roll to Build the Adventure

**Convention organizer is:**
1. Cunning and Sly / 2. Greedy and Wicked / 3. Clueless and Exploitable
4. Maybe too obsessed with honey / 5. A spoilt trust-fund kid / 6. Ruthless and Corrupt

**HoneyCon is being held in a** _(roll twice — adjective + location)_:

Adjective: 1. Creepy / 2. Busy / 3. Run-Down / 4. Beautiful / 5. Dangerous / 6. Lavish

Location: 1. Lakeside Camp / 2. Fishing Village / 3. Metropolitan City
4. Convention Centre / 5. Truck Convoy / 6. Wilderness Retreat

**Aside from loads of honey, the prize is:**
1. Ultradense megahoney from especially posh bees
2. A briefcase of pure manuka extract worth $5m
3. The Queen of All Bees, Once Exiled, Now Returned
4. Black Orchid Honey, which turns anyone who eats it into a goth
5. Abraham Lincoln's beehive, thought to be haunted by his ghost
6. Miss Universe 2017 — an especially attractive bee

**Security features** _(roll twice)_:
1. Armed Guards / 2. Electronically locked doors / 3. Laser tripwire grids
4. CCTV network / 5. "Impenetrable" Vault / 6. Poison Gas

**But little do the bears know...**
1. This place is rigged to blow! / 2. The cops are en route! / 3. Look — a rival team of bears!
4. They've been set up! / 5. The prize is a fake! / 6. The bees are angry!

## Supplies

* A bag of d6s — two per player (BEAR die + CRIMINAL die), plus rolling dice
* Story dice — used for random catastrophe, or if the team has earned a random power
* Lego minifigs for disguises
* Buildable creatures for sidekicks
* Foreign coins for loot / rewards
